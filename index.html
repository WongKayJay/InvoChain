How It Works

🧾

1) Issue

Invoice is created and verified with buyers (APIs/portals). A hash is anchored on‑chain to prevent double‑pledge.

💧

2) Fund

SMEs receive funding. Repayments are escrowed and tracked. Transparent risk grades guide investor allocations.

✅

3) Pay

Buyer settles the invoice. On‑chain status moves to paid, creating a complete audit trail for SMEs and investors.

About Invochain

Invochain helps small and medium enterprises (SMEs) access liquidity by verifying invoices on blockchain, preventing fraud, and building trust with transparent audit trails.

From origination to settlement, every step is anchored on‑chain — enabling faster approvals, escrow‑backed repayments, and real‑time status tracking. Investors gain transparency through public dashboards and risk grading by cohort.
